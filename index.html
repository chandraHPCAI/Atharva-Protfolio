About This STEM Portfolio

I am a 10th-grade student with a strong passion for robotics and engineering, built through over six years of competitive robotics experience. I started in VEX IQ and progressed into VEX V5, consistently competing at a high level and contributing to a team that achieved State Championship success and multiple regional qualifications.

My primary focus is on CAD design, mechanical systems, and supporting programming integration. I design and iterate mechanisms such as drivetrains, intakes, and lift systems in CAD before building, which helps improve reliability and performance during testing and competition. Through this process, I have developed strong problem-solving skills and a structured engineering approach based on iteration and continuous improvement.

In addition to robotics, I have worked on independent engineering projects that expand my technical skills. I developed an autonomous racing drone controller using PID calibration, built a high-acceleration 3D printing system powered by a Raspberry Pi cluster, and am currently working on a drone swarm system using machine learning for infrared-based disaster response. These projects have strengthened my understanding of control systems, embedded computing, and real-world engineering applications.

Alongside my technical work, I have maintained a strong academic record with a GPA close to 3.9 while taking challenging coursework. I have also developed leadership and communication skills through team collaboration, public speaking, and competitive events such as DECA and hackathons.

I am motivated to continue growing as an engineer by applying my skills to real-world challenges. I am especially interested in opportunities that connect robotics, aerospace, and advanced engineering systems, where I can contribute, learn, and create meaningful impact through hands-on innovation.
